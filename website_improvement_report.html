High Priority Recommendations

In cheezon ko sab se pehle karein. Inka impact sab se zyada hoga sales, rankings, aur trust par.

1. Add Open Graph & Schema to All Pages

Problem: Sirf homepage pe Open Graph aur structured data hai. About, Contact, Warranty, Privacy, go-pt-plus, aur category pages pe missing hai.

Impact: Social sharing aur Google rich results mein disadvantage hai.

Fix: Har page pe og:title, og:description, og:image, og:url, twitter:card tags add karein. About/Contact pages pe Organization/LocalBusiness schema add karein.

2. Optimize Video Files (144MB Total)

Problem: videos/ folder 144MB ka hai. Large videos mobile users ke liye problem hain.

Impact: Slow page load → higher bounce rate → lower conversions.

Fix: Videos compress karein (HandBrake use kar ke). YouTube/Vimeo pe upload kar ke embed karein. Agar self-host karna hai, toh 720p max rakhein.

3. Add Customer Reviews/Testimonials Section

Problem: Website pe real customer reviews display nahi hote. Trust kam banta hai.

Impact: Reviews se conversion rate 2-4x improve ho sakta hai.

Fix: Homepage pe "Customer Reviews" section add karein. Product pages pe bhi reviews dikhayein. Admin panel se reviews manage karne ka option add karein.

4. Add Lead Capture Forms (Email/WhatsApp)

Problem: Sirf WhatsApp order ka option hai. Visitors ka contact info collect karne ka koi tareeqa nahi.

Impact: Abandoned visitors follow-up nahi ho sakte.

Fix: "Get Best Price" ya "Request a Call" form add karein. Exit-intent popup add karein: "Enter your WhatsApp number for exclusive discount."

5. Improve Product Page Content

Problem: Product pages pe detailed specifications, comparison tables, FAQs, aur buying guides missing hain.

Impact: Customers ke sawalat jawab nahi hote, isliye conversion kam hoti hai.

Fix: Har product ke liye detailed content add karein: specs table, use cases, FAQ section, comparison with other models.
